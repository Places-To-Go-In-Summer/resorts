the clement hotel - all inclusive

Hotel Details

An 8-minute walk from the Palo Alto Caltrain station, this sleek, all-inclusive hotel is a 9-minute walk from Stanford Stadium on the Stanford University campus. Featuring wood furnishings, the contemporary suites offer free Wi-Fi and room service. They also have smart TVs, Nespresso machines and minifridges, in addition to separate living rooms and stone bathrooms. Meals and drinks are included and are served in a chic modern restaurant with an open kitchen. There's also a rooftop pool, a hot tub and a sundeck with cabanas, plus a gym and a 24/7 self-service pantry. Valet parking is free. 4.9/5 (Exceptional Reviews!).

hotel whitcomb

Hotel Details

This historic hotel that served as City Hall immediately following the 1906 earthquake is a 2-minute walk from the Asian Art Museum and a 5-minute walk from both the Civic Center and a BART rail station, and is served by a streetcar that runs along Market Street. Contemporary rooms and suites offer free Wi-Fi, desks and flat-screen TVs; a suite features a living room, dining area and wet bar. The ornate high-ceilinged lobby exhibits marble columns, crystal chandeliers and Tiffany stained glass. Amenities include an Asian fusion restaurant, a coffee shop and a wine bar, as well an Internet cafe, meeting spaces and an exercise room. 3.5/5 (Good Reviews)

the meritage resort and spa

Hotel Details

In a commercial area off Highway 221, this relaxed resort is 2 miles from the Napa River and 5 miles from the Starmont Winery & Vineyards. Warmly decorated rooms come with free Wi-Fi and a bottle of wine on arrival, as well as flat-screen TVs, minifridges, and tea and coffeemakers. Upgraded rooms add sitting areas, and furnished terraces or balconies offering vineyard views. Room service is available. Dining options include a stylish restaurant and 2 bars. There's also a cafe, a spa and a gym, plus an outdoor pool with a terrace and sunloungers. Pets are welcome. 4/5 (Very Good Reviews!)
